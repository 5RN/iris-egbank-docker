PERSONAL LOANS

Let your dreams come true with EGBANK Personal Loans

Benefits

  • Loan amount can reach up to EGP 3,000
  • Loan tenure from 6 months up to 120 months
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Life insurance throughout loan tenure

Requirements

  • Copy of valid national ID
  • Recent utility bill (within 3 months)
  • Proof of income:
    • Salary / Installment transfer customers: Agreement with employer to transfer salary / installments.
    • Non-Salary / Installment transfer customers: Authenticated HR letter or HR letter with bank statements from the past 3 months.
    • Self-employed / Professional customers: Valid tax card and last 6 months of bank statements, in addition to copy of recent and valid commercial register for self-employed or certificate of profession, valid syndicate ID and medical institution license for professionals.

Eligibility

  • Salaried clients: Minimum age of 21 years up to 60 years at loan maturity.
  • Self-employed and Professionals: Up to 65 years of age at loan maturity.
